* To update local Strapi database:
1. Log in to AWS SSO
2. aws s3 cp --profile wisp-tech-dev s3://wisp-strapi-data-import-export/latest.sql .
3. dropdb local_strapi
4. createdb local_strapi
5. psql -d local_strapi < latest.sql
